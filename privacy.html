Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 8, 2025

At Carbi, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our car matching service, we collect:

  • Contact details: First name, last name, email address, phone number
  • Location data: Postcode or town, and optionally your device location coordinates
  • Car preferences: Budget, brand preferences, colour preferences, mileage requirements, and other features important to you
  • Additional information: Any notes or specific requirements you provide

Information We Collect Automatically

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, and interaction with forms
  • Device information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Cookies: We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Cookie Policy below)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with suitable cars from local dealers
  • Contact you via your preferred method (email, text, or phone call)
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Send you updates about your car search (if you've opted in)

3. Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us understand how you use our service and improve your experience.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., storing your form progress)
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics helps us understand how visitors use our site
  • Marketing cookies: Meta Pixel and Google Ads help us figure out where to advertise

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use some features of our website.

4. Sharing Your Information

We only share your information:

  • With car dealers: When we've found a potential match, we share relevant details with trusted local dealers
  • With service providers: We use Supabase for data storage, Meta and Google for advertising
  • When required by law: If legally obligated to disclose information

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security reviews

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@carbi.co

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our service:

  • Active searches: Until your car search is completed or you request deletion
  • Completed/archived searches: Up to 2 years for service improvement
  • Marketing data: Until you unsubscribe or request deletion

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is designed to help young drivers (typically 17+), but requires parental involvement. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by email or through a notice on our website.

10. Contact Us

  • Email: hello@carbi.co
